Newcomb-Benford Stats

This is a program that analyses a given dataset, checking if it exhibits a very strong/strong/weak/no Benford relationship by plugging in the numbers into various mathematical formulae. It first performs basic statistical analysis on the data, before moving into Newcomb-Benford Law specific formulae. Also includes a graph that adjusts it's scaling based on the maximum frequency in the entire data set.
